# Storie

> Sell what you make. Keep what you earn.

Niche storefronts for potters, bakers, fitness coaches, and authors. Zero platform fees. One photo to start.

## What it does

- **Single-photo setup** — Your store goes live in under sixty seconds. Literally.
- **Zero platform fees** — Stripe fees only. You keep the rest of every sale.
- **Own your customers** — Email addresses, reorders, loyalty. No middleman in between.

## Why

Creator commerce. Part of a 20-product exploration of high-demand consumer and SMB markets.

## Status

Landing page live. Product in planning.

- **Live**: https://mukundakatta.github.io/storie/

## Stack (when built)
